Future improvements

I have several axes for GHDL improvements:

  • Documentation.
  • Better diagnostics messages (warning and error).
  • Full support of VHDL-87 and VHDL-93.
  • Support of VHDL-02.
  • Optimization (simulation speed).
  • Graphical tools (to see waves and to debug)
  • Style checks
  • VITAL acceleration
