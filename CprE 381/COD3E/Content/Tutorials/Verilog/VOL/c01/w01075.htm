Given the following module: 


	module modB (port1, port2, port3, port4);
		input port1, port2;
		inout port3;
		output port4;
		...
		parameter par1 = 1,
			par2 = 2,
			par3 = par1+par2;
		...
	endmodule
Indicate whether the following instances of modB above are legal or illegal.


a. Legal   Illegal modB #(7,8) mb2 (p4, p2, p3, q4);
b. Legal   Illegal modB mb2 (p4, p2, p3, q4);
c. Legal   Illegal modB (p1, p2, p3, p4);
These are the correct answers.
