The module definition by itself does not create a module. Modules are created by being instantiated in another module, like this: 

        module <module_name_1> (<portlist>);
                	.
                	.       
               <module_name_2> <instance_name> (<portlist>);
                	.
                	.
        endmodule



There is one exception to the above rule, and that is for the top level modules.
