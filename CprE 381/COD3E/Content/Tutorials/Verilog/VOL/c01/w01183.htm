Are the following statements syntactically correct?

a. Yes   No     assign x = f(y), y = f(z);

b. Yes   No     assign #10 x <= a + b;

The second statement is incorrect because you can't use a non-blocking assign. Here is an example of a correct statement for b.:
b. assign #10 x = a + b;
