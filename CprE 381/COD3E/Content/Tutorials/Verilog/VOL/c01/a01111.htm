Are the following statements syntactically correct?


a. Yes   No and #1 a1 (a, b, c);
b. Yes   No and (a,b,c), (d,e,f);
c. Yes   No AND #1 (a, b, c);
Correct. The last statement is incorrect. Here are two examples of correct statements for c.:
c. and #1 (a,b,c);
or
c. AND #1 myand (a,b,c);
Note that "AND #1 myand (a,b,c);" instantiates a module which is not a primitive.
