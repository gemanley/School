Indicate which of the following are legal operands.


Legal   Illegal abc
Legal   Illegal $time
Legal   Illegal $monitor
Legal   Illegal #1
Legal   Illegal 123
Legal   Illegal 1'bx

 

That's right. The statement "$monitor" is a task, not a function, so it can't be used in an expression. Also, "#1" is a delay expression, not an operand.
