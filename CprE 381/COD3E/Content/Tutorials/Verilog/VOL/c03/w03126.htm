Given 
	parameter p1 = 4;
	reg [4:0] x;
	wire [3:2] y;
	reg z;
Classify these expressions as "constant", "scalar", "delay", "fixed-size", or "non-fixed-size":

These are the correct answers:

1. p1-1 constant
2. #x delay
3. x != p1 scalar
4. z scalar
5. x*y non-fixed-size
6. x & y fixed-size
