Signals are combined on a net by having two (or more) primitives with the same net as output. For example,

buf (strong1,pull0) b1(Result, A);
buf (strong1,pull0) b2(Result, B);
When unambiguous signals are combined on a net, the result is either unambiguous 0, unambiguous 1, or ambiguous x.
