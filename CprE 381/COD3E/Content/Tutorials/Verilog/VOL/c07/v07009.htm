Modelling clocks in Verilog is surprisingly complicated, considering that a clock just oscillates between 0 and 1. There are lots of ways of making such an oscillator. These are the most obvious:

  • Primitive (Gate Level) 
        not #period/2 (clk, clk);
  • Continuous Assignment 
        assign #period/2 clk = ~clk;
  • Procedural 
        always #period/2
            clk = ~clk;
