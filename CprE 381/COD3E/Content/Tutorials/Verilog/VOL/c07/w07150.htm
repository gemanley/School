What is the definition of a race condition?

1. A race condition occurs when a signal is sampled and modified at the same instant of simulated time.
2. A race condition is a violation of a module path timing check.
3. A race condition occurs when a loop is executed with no included time or event control.
4. A race condition occurs when two signals are sampled at the same instant of simulated time.
The correct answer is Statement 1. To review this topic, see Race Conditions.
