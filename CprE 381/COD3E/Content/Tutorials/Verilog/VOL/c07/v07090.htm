We have already defined a race condition to be a situation where the behavior of the model depends on the order of execution of events at the same simulated time. (See Defined vs. Undefined Behavior (Races) in Chapter 3.)

In general, a race condition occurs when a data value is sampled in one process and changed in another at the same simulated time.

A simple race is:

	initial				initial
	   #100 a = 1;			   #100 if(a) $display("a is 1");
The $display statement may or may not be executed, depending on the order of execution of the two events at time 100.
