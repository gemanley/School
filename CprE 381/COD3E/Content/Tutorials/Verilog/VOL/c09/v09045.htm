The TF routines are so-called because they are used with user-defined tasks and functions. These were the original utility routines in the PLI, and as such are considerably more limited in functionality than the later ACC routines.

In general, the TF routines allow you to get and put the values of arguments to the user-defined task or function. The utility routines can only manipulate registers or nets which appear in the argument list, and no others.
