A user-written routine needs to be called at an appropriate time during the simulation. The PLI defines a set of times at which a user-written routine may be called.

  1. at compile time
  2. when the user-defined task/function is called in the Verilog source
  3. at miscellaneous times:
    end of simulation
    change of value of an argument
    end of a time step
    simulation event scheduled by tf_setdelay
    execution of $stop
    execution of $save
    execution of $restart
