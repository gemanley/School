Given the following Verilog module and associated pli.tab:

	module test;

		reg [127:0] in1, in2;
		reg clk;
		wire [127:0] out1, out2;
		BigChip bc1 (in1, in2, out1, out2, clk);

		integer nets;
		initial begin
		nets = $countNets(bc1);
		$display("BigChip has %d nets", nets);
		end
	endmodule

pli.tab

$countNets  call=CountEm  size=32
Which pli routines would CountEm be most likely to use?

1. acc_handle_object, tf_putp, and acc_next_object
2. acc_next_net, tf_putp, and acc_fetch_value
3. tf_arg, tf_putp, and acc_next_net
4. acc_handle_tfarg, tf_putp, and acc_next_net
5. all of the above
The correct answer is item 4.
