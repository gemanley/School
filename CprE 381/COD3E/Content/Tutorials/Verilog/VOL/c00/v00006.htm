This course contains several buttons to help you navigate:

As you've seen, the Next button takes you to the next screen.
The Previous button takes you the previous screen.
The Table of Contents button links to the Table of Contents screen.
The Chapter Overview button takes you to an outline of the current chapter. You can click within the outline to go directly to the appropriate section of the chapter.
The Back button allows you to jump back after you have followed a link to a different section of the course. It is only enabled after you have gone to a screen that is out of the usual course sequence.
