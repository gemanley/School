OBJECTIVES

On completion of this chapter you will be able to:

  • state the relationships among gate, register transfer, and behavioral level modelling
  • identify the modelling structures that are used for behavioral and RTL modelling, including statement controls, procedural assignments, control statements, and compound statements
  • correct the syntax for each of the modelling structures that are used for behavioral and RTL modelling
  • write a behavioral or register transfer level module to produce a given output
  • predict the output of a given behavioral or register transfer level module
