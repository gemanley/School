There are three variations of the case statement in Verilog. They have the form: 

        case  (selector) case_item {case_item} endcase
or      casex (selector) case_item {case_item} endcase
or      casez (selector) case_item {case_item} endcase
where
        case_item is 
                item {, item} : statement_or_null
           or   default : statement_or_null

        selector and item are expressions
