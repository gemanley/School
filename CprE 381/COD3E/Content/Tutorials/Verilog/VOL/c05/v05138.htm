The sequential block is just the familiar sequence of statements bracketed by begin and end. The statements in the sequence are executed in order, though they may include all of the time or event controls, and may even include parallel blocks.

Examples:

begin
     x = f(y);
#10  y = z;
     do_vector;
end

begin
     indx = 1;
     while (indx < 10)
          begin
               vec[indx] = vec[indx-1];
               indx = indx+1;
          end
end
