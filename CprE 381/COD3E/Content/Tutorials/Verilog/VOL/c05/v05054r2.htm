The race can also be solved with an intra-assignment event control: 

    always @(posedge clock)             always @(posedge clock)
        dff1 = @(negedge clock) f(x);       dff2 = @(negedge clock) dff1;

The execution sequence of events is:

1. wait until the rising edge of clock
2&3. evaluate f(x) and save
evaluate dff1 and save
4. wait until the falling edge of clock
5&6. assign saved f(x) to dff1
assign saved dff1 to dff2

Again, dff2 will get the value of dff1 which it has at the posedge of clock.
