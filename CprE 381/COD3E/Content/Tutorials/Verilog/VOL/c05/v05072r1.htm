This race can be fixed by the use of non-blocking assignment: 

    always @(posedge clock)         always @(posedge clock)
        dff1 
			
			
			<= f(x); dff2 <= dff1;
The execution sequence of events is:

1. wait until the rising edge of clock
2&3. evaluate f(x) and save
evaluate dff1 and save
4. wait until all other events have executed
5&6. assign saved f(x) to dff1
assign saved dff1 to dff2

This reliably solves the problem.
