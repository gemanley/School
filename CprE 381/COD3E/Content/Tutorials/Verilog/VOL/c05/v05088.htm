Write a gate version of the following assign statement. Click Answer to see a solution. 
	wire [1:0] s;
	wire a, x, y, z;
	assign #1 a = s==1 ? x : s==2 ? y : z;

One possible solution is:
	or  #1 mux (a, t1, t2, t3);
	not (s0_, s[0]);
	not (s1_, s[1]);
	and (sel1, s1_, s[0]);
	and (sel2, s[1], s0_);
	nor (sel3, sel1, sel2);
	and (t1, sel1, x);
	and (t2, sel2, y);
	and (t3, sel3, z);
