Which of the following statements are legal?

1. wire aBc;
2. wire \reg\;
3. wire ABC;
4. wire abc1;
5. wire 1abc;
6. wire $abc1;

Statements 2, 5, and 6 are illegal. Statement 2 is illegal because an escaped identifier must end with space. So the ";" is part of the identifier. Statements 5 and 6 are illegal because the first character of an identifier can't be numeric or a "$".
