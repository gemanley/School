For both built-in primitives and continuous assignments, the strength is associated with the driver, and it consists of one or two keywords, like this:

(strength0, strength1)
or
(strength1, strength0)

strength0 and strength1 are keywords, which can have the following values:

strength0 strength1
supply0 supply1
strong0 strong1
pull0 pull1
weak0 weak1
highz0 highz1
