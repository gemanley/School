Before you enter this site, please observe the following technical requirements:

  • Zoom Viewer plug-in from IseeMedia
  • 800x600 resolution or better
  • Maximize your browser window
  • Bandwidth considerations: file size vary from approx. 100k to 20k

This site is comprised of 25 photographic panoramas of Chavín de Huántar which are navigated by clicking and dragging. "Hot Spots" are links in the photograph which lead you to a panorama of that location. Basic instructions are as follows:

Keyboard Options Cursor Indications
Zoom In Click for object view and information
Zoom Out Click and drag to navigate
Show Hot Spots Click to move to next viewpoint
