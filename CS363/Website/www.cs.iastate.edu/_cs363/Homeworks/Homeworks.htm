Collaboration on homeworks

Collaboration on the homeworks is allowed so long as it is limited to your colleagues who are currently registered in Com S 363. Your objective should be to learn, because if you only play a passive role in your study group, you will perhaps be ill prepared for the tests. 

Due dates for homeworks

Due dates are listed in the course time table under [Course Info] web page. Homeworks are due at the beginning of the class. For our policy on late submissions, please see the course syllabus.
