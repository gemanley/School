The best way to prepare for the test is to have completed homeworks and projects that are due before the test. You should go through your and our solutions to the homeworks and also go over the projects. The purpose of the sample tests is to familiarize you with the overall format and style. We try that relative to the preparation of the students, the overall difficulty of the tests remain a constant from test to test during and across semesters. Some of the problems in this sample test from past have been incorporated in course lectures now. You should not expect any specific amount of overlap with material on lectures.
