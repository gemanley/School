Setting up and using Secure Shell for your Unix account

Go to [ http://tech.its.iastate.edu/win2000/downloads/downloads.shtml ] and download Secure Shell and install it.

Start Secure Shell, you will see the following screen. 

You need to configure it for your use. Click on [Quick Connect], You will see the following screen.

Enter pyrite.cs.iastate.edu as hostname and your CS account user name. Leave the Port Number as is. Press [Connect]. Provide your password when asked. You will see the following window.
 

You can now enter commands and execute them.

You can also use Secure Shell to transfer files between your machine and your Unix account. To do this, click on the icon for "New File Transfer Window". The icon is on the toolbar just left of the "Settings" icon with gears. You will see the following window from where you can transfer files.
